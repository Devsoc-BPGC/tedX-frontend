About Us

one of India's oldest and biggest student run TEDx conference

Having a legacy passed on for years, becoming bigger and better with each passing edition

TEDx brings the spirit of TED’s mission of ideas worth spreading to local communities around the globe. TEDx events are organised independently under a free license granted by TED and include live speakers and recorded TED talks. Our event is called TEDxBITSGoa, where x = independently organized TED event.

  • An exclusive team of undergraduates from BITS Pilani KK Birla Goa Campus.
  • Featured people such as Kiran Bedi, Kalki Koechlin, Clinton Cerejo, Sonam Wangchuk, Sumeet Vyas and many others.
